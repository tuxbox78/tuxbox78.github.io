HealthBrowser – Privacy Policy

HealthBrowser accesses Apple HealthKit data solely to display and export it directly on your device. No health data is ever transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service of any kind.

All data processing happens entirely on-device. HealthBrowser makes zero network requests. There is no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, no advertising framework, and no account system included in the app.

The app requires only the HealthKit read permission that you explicitly grant via the standard iOS permission dialog. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

No data is collected, stored externally, or shared — ever.
